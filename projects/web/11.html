Folly Beach Child Care

I love Folly Beach. There is something about driving across that bridge on to the island that melts away the stress. Folly Beach Child Care's owner, Molly Byers, understands our beloved island and noticed a gap in the marketplace. After having a child herself, she decided that visitors to Folly needed a break from the toddlers to truly experience all that this island has to offer. I was happy to help her with this project from the ground up. We developed a strong logo, branding & website to match her target market.
