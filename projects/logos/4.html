Green Flash Guesthouses

If you a looking for a place to stay in Charleston, look no further than Green Flash Guesthouses. Located on James Island, they are in only 5 minutes to Folly Beach and 10 minutes to downtown Charleston. I developed this patch style logo for Green Flash to begin establishing their brand in the market. We wanted a worn looking logo with an anchor, green burst & guesthouses.
