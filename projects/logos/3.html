Chico Feo

"Tacos. Beers. Beards." Chico Feo's owner Hank Week laughed his ass off at my slogan for his new concept on Folly Beach. This place is like a treehouse for grown ups equipt with fresh fish tacos, PBR and drunken conversations about waves ridden. Stop on by and visit them, right across from Bert's Market.
